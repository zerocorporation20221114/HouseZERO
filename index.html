Your Green Retreat, Away from the City Buzz

Nestled in a quiet residential neighborhood in Tokyo, this entirely renovated traditional Japanese house blends the warmth of classic architecture with modern comforts.

Surrounded by rivers and parks, the area is serene enough to spot fireflies in early summer. Relax in the spacious living room and enjoy quality time with family or friends.

Why Stay Here

① Walking Distance to ICU, ASIJ & More

Conveniently close to Tokyo University of Foreign Studies, ICU (International Christian University), and ASIJ (American School in Japan). Also ideal for attending events at Ajinomoto Stadium.

② Spacious 60m² · Up to 6 Guests

A generous 15-tatami LDK plus two separate bedrooms (Western & Japanese style) and two toilets mean even large groups can move around comfortably in the morning without stress.

③ Long-Stay Ready · Free Parking

Full kitchen, individual A/C units, washer/dryer, and high-speed Wi-Fi. One free parking space on the property makes it perfect for road trips or business travel.
