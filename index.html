Escape the Hustle & Bustle to a Green Hideaway

Nestled in a quiet residential area of Tokyo, this property is a fully detached house that has been beautifully renovated down to every detail, while preserving the nostalgic charm of a traditional Japanese residence.

The surrounding area features scenic rivers and parks—you can even see fireflies in early summer. It offers a peaceful and natural setting. Enjoy a relaxing and memorable time with your family or friends in our spacious living area.

Why Choose Us

① Great Access to Universities & Stadium

Close to Tokyo University of Foreign Studies (TUFS), International Christian University (ICU), and The American School in Japan (ASIJ). It is perfect for families or friends visiting students. Also highly recommended for those attending events at Ajinomoto Stadium.

② Spacious 60㎡ Entire House (Max 6 Guests)

Features a huge 24㎡ (15-tatami) LDK, plus two independent bedrooms (one Western, one Japanese style). With 2 toilets available, morning preparations are smooth and stress-free even for large groups or families.

③ Perfect for Long Stays & Free Parking

Equipped with a full kitchen, ductless individual AC, in-unit washer/dryer, and high-speed Wi-Fi. A free parking space is available on-site (for 1 car), making it extremely convenient for sightseeing or business trips by car.
